About Us

We are The Project Podcast. Wether you have a certain Topic or Guest that you like to listen to,
our platform will make it much easier to find and explore these topics.
We believe In keeping people together by just listening to each other.

After taking a quick tour of our site, feel free to sign-up for free, create an account and the rest is all for you to enjoy.
If you are new to the world of podcast, don’t worry! You can add topics and guests to your likings.
If you are still unsure on what to listen to, that’s fine, This way our smart interface will give you
up to five different topics and guest to try out.
